Our compromise:

natural ingredients

When developing new products we work with cosmetic scientists to ensure that the ingredients used are naturally derived, and we ensure that we steer well clear of any harsh chemicals and certainly controversial ingredients.

vegan

All of our products are cruelty-free. We do not test our products or ingredients on animals and we do not retail in any country that requires animal testing.

paraben-free

None of our range of cosmetics contains parabens, fragrances or fillers. Instead we use safer, more natural chemicals.

eco-friendly

Our packaging is 100% recyclable and biodegradable. Some of the materials we use are: Bamboo Fibre, Polylactic Acid (BioPlastic) and Paper.

pre-life

Pre Life benefits are the environmental benefits of manufacturing our products, i.e. before the consumer uses the product.

Raw materials used in the production of our products are abundant and renewable, sustainably managed and plant based. There are also significantly less greenhouse gases emmitted during production.
